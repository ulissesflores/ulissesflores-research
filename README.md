# Ulisses Flores Research

Research hub for papers, datasets, software artifacts, and reproducible academic outputs.

This repository is the umbrella landing page for my academic and research-adjacent work. It points to the software artifacts, paper workspaces, and citation-ready repositories that support my research in AI systems, forecasting, resilience, and complex environments.

## What lives here

- Paper workspaces and manuscript support files
- Links to flagship software artifacts maintained in dedicated repositories
- A stable navigation layer for research outputs, releases, and academic references

## Flagship software artifacts

- [llm-contextizer](https://github.com/ulissesflores/llm-contextizer) - Zero-dependency Python CLI for turning codebases into token-efficient contexts for LLMs and research workflows.
- [mit508-techgrowth-des](https://github.com/ulissesflores/mit508-techgrowth-des) - Discrete-event simulation of Kafka-Flink-Iceberg pipelines under PACELC and network-partition stress.
- [cyberfinancial-resilience-lrblstm](https://github.com/ulissesflores/cyberfinancial-resilience-lrblstm) - Research software for cyber-financial resilience modeling with stochastic systems, Little's Law, and LSTM forecasting.
- [odisseo-signal-atlas](https://github.com/ulissesflores/odisseo-signal-atlas) - Multilingual GitHub discovery engine for frontier engineering, open-source signals, and agentic search.
- [mirofish-citation-edition](https://github.com/ulissesflores/mirofish-citation-edition) - Citation-focused archival edition of MiroFish with DOI, multilingual documentation, and scholarly metadata.

## Research principles

- Reproducible software and versioned releases
- Clear authorship, provenance, and citation practices
- Public-facing documentation for technical and academic audiences
- Separation between umbrella research navigation and repository-specific implementation details
